# Outbound Sequence Template

## Email 1 - Cold Open (Day 0)

**Subject**: [Short, curiosity-driven, no clickbait]

[Name],

Hope you're doing well!

[1 sentence referencing something specific about their company - a recent hire, product launch, or public statement.]

[1 sentence connecting that to a problem you solve.]

[1 sentence with a soft CTA - question, not a pitch.]

[Your name]

---

## Email 2 - Value Add (Day 3)

**Subject**: Re: [Previous subject]

[Name],

[Share something genuinely useful - a benchmark, a case study, a relevant data point. No pitch.]

[1 sentence connecting the value to their situation.]

[Soft CTA - "Would this be useful for your team?"]

[Your name]

---

## Email 3 - Social Proof (Day 7)

**Subject**: [Company similar to theirs] + [your company]

[Name],

[1 sentence about a customer in their space and the result they got.]

[1 sentence about what made it work.]

[Direct CTA - "Worth a 15-min look?"]

[Your name]

---

## Email 4 - Breakup (Day 14)

**Subject**: Closing the loop

[Name],

[1 sentence acknowledging silence - no guilt trip.]

[1 sentence leaving the door open.]

[Your name]

---

## Notes

- Personalize email 1 heavily - the first line should prove you did your homework
- Keep every email under 100 words
- Track reply rates per email to optimize the sequence
