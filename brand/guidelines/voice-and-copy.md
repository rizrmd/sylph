# Voice & Copy Guidelines

## Brand Positioning

pnv is a browser as a service — a humble tool that just works, is always reliable, and can be trusted upon.

### Core Messaging Hierarchy

1. **One-liner:** pnv is a browser as a service.
2. **What it does:** Spin up cloud-hosted browser instances from your browser. Interact transparently. Mark any past interaction and replay it as an automated task.
3. **The problem:** Existing browser automation is either local-only or API-only. pnv gives you a full, visible cloud browser you can control remotely.

### Logo Taglines

Short taglines for use with the pnv logo/wordmark (2–5 words ideal, fragments OK). All follow brand voice: direct, technical, humble, no hype.

**Primary recommendations:**
- **"Replay exactly."** — strongest, owns the core differentiator
- **"A browser as a service."** — official one-liner, clean for lockups
- **"No code. No AI. Exact."** — clear contrast vs competitors

**Strong alternatives:**
- "Interact. Replay. Repeat."
- "Record once. Replay forever."
- "Your browser, replayed."

### Brand Beliefs

- Be the tool that quietly works — no hype, no drama
- Transparency builds trust: show exactly what's happening
- No AI, no guessing, no hallucination. Every task is deterministic.
- Automation shouldn't be brittle — replayed tasks should just work

## Voice Characteristics

### Tone Spectrum

| Attribute | We ARE | We are NOT |
|-----------|--------|------------|
| Confident | Direct, assured, reliable | Arrogant, hype-y, boastful |
| Technical | Precise, specific, practitioner-level | Jargon-heavy, gatekeeping |
| Humble | Understated, earned confidence | Self-deprecating, apologetic |
| Honest | Clear, direct, transparent | Slick, marketing-speak |
| Concise | Every word earns its place | Terse, cold, incomplete |

### Writing Rules

**Product name casing**
- Correct: "pnv does X"
- Wrong: "PNV", "Pnv", "Ponovo"

**Key terms - use precisely**
- cloud browser: a full browser instance running in the cloud, controllable remotely
- replayable task: a marked sequence of browser interactions saved as an automated workflow
- interaction: any user action in the cloud browser (click, type, navigate, scroll)
- session: a cloud browser instance from spin up to termination

**Sentence structure**
- Prefer short, declarative sentences
- Fragments are fine when they land
- Use dashes (-) for asides
- No Oxford comma (stay lean)

**Active voice**
- Yes: "Spin up a cloud browser from your browser"
- No: "A cloud browser can be spun up from your browser"

### Word Choices

**Prefer:**
- "reliable" over "robust"
- "simple" over "easy"
- "works" over "delivers"
- "uses" over "leverages"
- "show" over "surface"
- "trusted" over "enterprise-grade"

**Avoid:**
- "leverage", "utilize", "synergize", "empower"
- "best-in-class", "cutting-edge", "next-generation"
- "seamless" (overused)
- "robust" (vague)
- "solution" as a noun (unless referring to a specific technical solution)
- "revolutionary", "game-changing", "disruptive"
- Any superlatives you wouldn't say to another engineer

## Copy Formats

### Headlines
- Short. 2-5 words ideal.
- Can break grammar rules for impact (fragments OK)
- Lead with the verb when possible

**Pattern:** [Verb] your [thing].

### Product Descriptions
- Lead with what it does, not what it is
- One sentence = one idea
- End with the benefit, not the feature

**Pattern:** [Action verb] your [thing] — [benefit].

### Feature Copy
- Start with the verb
- Stay under 15 words per feature line
- No periods at the end of feature list items

### Social Media
- Direct and technical. You're talking to builders.
- Share what you're building, not press releases.
- Exclamation marks: sparingly, max one per post.

## Describing Your Product

When writing about what pnv does, organize around these pillars:

1. **Cloud browsers** — full browser instances in the cloud, controllable from any browser
2. **Transparent interaction** — see everything happening in real time, nothing hidden
3. **Replayable tasks** — mark past interactions, replay them as automated workflows

Never position pnv as "yet another automation tool" or "another AI browser agent." pnv is the tool that quietly and reliably runs your browser workflows — no local machine needed, no blind scripts, no AI hallucination risk.
